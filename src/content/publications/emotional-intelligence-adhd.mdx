abstract: |
  A longitudinal study (N = 187) of an ADHD-focused workplace training programme measured
  at T1–T4 over eighteen months. Repeated-measures ANOVA reveals emotional intelligence as
  the most responsive variable to intervention, with downstream effects on perceived employability
  and subjective wellbeing.
