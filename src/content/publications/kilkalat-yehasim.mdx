abstract: |
  This Hebrew-language monograph maps the dark side of interpersonal interactions at work.
  Its title plays on the phonetic closeness of kalkalat yehasim ("the economy of relationships")
  and kilkalat yehasim ("the ruining of relationships") — arguing that the two are
  structurally linked. Across its chapters, the book synthesises two decades of research on
  incivility, bullying, harassment, and abusive supervision, and offers practical implications
  for managers, HR practitioners, and OD consultants in Israel and beyond.
